# Spot illustration

Template uses a two-part structure. Sections named "Subject" and "Negative prompt"
are always sent. Sections named "Style" and "Style negatives" are only sent when
no reference image is provided.

## Subject

Composition: single idea expressed visually, tight focal area, confident negative space, lightly asymmetric, reads clearly at 600px wide.

Mood: clear, confident, quiet. A visual aside that reinforces a paragraph — not decoration.

## Style

Palette anchored by warm off-white background (#FAF7F2) with accents in deep navy (#0B1F3A) and one secondary accent (soft terracotta #D97757 OR muted sage #6B8068 — pick one that fits the concept, never both).
